How to Apply for Tax-Free Childcare and 30-Hour Free Childcare

Applying for Tax-Free Childcare and the 30-hour entitlement is done through the same HMRC portal in one application. Here's a clear step-by-step guide to the process.

Last updated: March 2025 · Based on 2024/25 HMRC rules

What you'll need before you start

  • ✓
    Your National Insurance number
  • ✓
    Your child's date of birth
  • ✓
    Details of your employer or self-employment
  • ✓
    Your Government Gateway login (or set one up — it takes 5 minutes)
  • ✓
    Your partner's details if applicable

Step-by-step application

  1. Go to gov.uk/tax-free-childcare
    This is the official HMRC portal. Don't use any third-party sites.
  2. Sign in or create a Government Gateway account
    If you don't have one, it takes about 5 minutes to set up. You'll need your National Insurance number and a form of ID verification.
  3. Apply for Tax-Free Childcare and/or 30 hours
    You can apply for both in the same application. HMRC will ask about your work situation, expected earnings, and your child's details.
  4. Receive your eligibility confirmation
    Most applications are approved instantly. You'll receive a childcare account number (for Tax-Free Childcare) and a 30-hour code (for the free hours).
  5. Give your 30-hour code to your provider
    Your nursery or childminder needs this code to claim the funded hours from the government. Give it to them well before the term starts.
  6. Top up your Tax-Free Childcare account
    Deposit money into your online account. The government top-up (20%) is added automatically, usually within 24 hours. Pay your provider directly from the account.
  7. Reconfirm every 3 months
    HMRC sends a reminder. Log in and confirm you're still eligible. It takes 2 minutes. Missing this window suspends your account.

Apply early: Apply at least 3 months before your child's eligibility date. Providers often need the code weeks before the term starts to allocate places.

How long does approval take?

Most online applications are approved instantly or within 24 hours. In some cases HMRC may request additional information, which can take a few days. Applications submitted close to the term start date may not be processed in time for the first term.

Frequently asked questions

How long does Tax-Free Childcare take to set up?

Most applications are approved instantly online. Once approved you can start depositing money and the government top-up appears within 24 hours.

Can I apply before my child is born?

No — you need your child's date of birth to apply. Apply as soon as your child is born and is approaching the eligible age.

What happens if I miss the 3-month reconfirmation?

Your account is suspended. You can reactivate it by logging in and completing the reconfirmation — but there may be a gap in your funded hours while the code is suspended.
